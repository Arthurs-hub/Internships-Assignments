About Karo Pitch

Bridging the Gap Between
Founders and Investors

Thousands of founders across India are building amazing businesses in Tier-2, Tier-3 cities and Bharat, but they lack access to investors, mentorship, and visibility.

Karo Pitch is designed to become India's most accessible startup discovery and funding platform. We create structured pitch events similar to closed-room investor meetings, where selected startups present their businesses directly to investors.
