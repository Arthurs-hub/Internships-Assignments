About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specializing in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments.

We bridge the gap between academic insight and real-world application through our partnership with leading UK universities. Our unique model combines rigorous training programs with hands-on project experience, ensuring our practitioners are equipped to tackle the most complex digital risk challenges facing organizations today.

By fostering collaboration between academia and industry, we create a pipeline of highly skilled professionals ready to address emerging threats in AI governance, cybersecurity, and digital resilience.
