# IDR Logo Design Explanation

## Design Choices

**Symbolism:**
The logo features a 3D cube/hexagon structure that represents the multifaceted nature of digital risk management. The geometric shape symbolizes structure, stability, and resilience - core values of the Institute. The three visible faces represent the three service pillars (Academy, Innovation, Advisory), while the intersecting lines at the center symbolize the convergence of academic insight and industry practice.

**Color Palette:**
The primary orange (#FF6B35) conveys energy, innovation, and forward-thinking approach to digital risk. The gradient to a lighter orange (#FF8C42) adds depth and modernity. Black (#0A0A0A) provides strong contrast and professionalism, while white ensures clean readability. This combination creates a tech-forward yet educational aesthetic suitable for both academic and corporate environments.

**Typography:**
Inter font family was chosen for its exceptional legibility at all sizes, modern geometric structure, and professional appearance. The bold weight (800) ensures the logo remains readable even at favicon sizes (16x16px), while the tight letter-spacing (-1) creates a cohesive, contemporary look that complements the geometric icon.

**Scalability:**
The logo is designed as a vector (SVG) ensuring perfect clarity at any size. The icon-only variant works perfectly for favicons, mobile headers, and social media avatars, while the full variant with text is ideal for website headers, presentations, and marketing materials.
